Dr Joe Abah is the Country Director of DAI. DAI is an
international development company that works with a
wide range of clients, including national and local
government, bilateral and multilateral donors, private
corporations, and philanthropies in over 100 countries.
Before joining DAI in September 2017, joe
was the Director-General of the Bureau of Public Service Refrorm
in Nigeria. Under his leardership, he emphasized the need
for public service reforms to focus on tangible
improvement in the lives of citizens, not just
administrative forms. His Bureau has successfully
intervened to make drivers licenses, passport, tax
clerance certificates and national identification number easier to process and obtain.
